Our idea :

Create a club in each big city of France with the same music at the same time and transmit what happens in the other nightclubs. As if France was partying all together. Everything should be the same as in a Mcdonald's.

How to choose?

The idea would be to have different rooms with different musical styles. Each participant will be able to choose the room he prefers in order to have fun according to his wishes. It is important to us that all our clients have the best time and have indelible memories. The DJ and drink menu will be available for you to choose from!
